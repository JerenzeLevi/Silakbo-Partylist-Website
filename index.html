Platforms

Under the banner of SILAKBO, our mission is distilled into one powerful, easy-to-remember acronym:

OPEN

OPEN ACCESS

We believe that trust is built on clarity. This platform ensures total transparency in all student government communications, financial transactions, and decision-making processes, keeping every student informed and involved.

OPEN HEARTS

Rooted in the spirit of Bayanihan, this platform focuses on compassionate service. Through initiatives like Tubig Sagip Piso and dedicated support for school projects, we aim to look out for one another and strengthen our community bond.

OPEN FOR IMPROVEMENT

We are looking toward the future. By prioritizing leadership empowerment and promoting a greener, sustainable community, we aim to implement lasting improvements that benefit both the students and the environment.

"OPEN ACCESS. OPEN HEARTS. OPEN FOR IMPROVEMENT."
